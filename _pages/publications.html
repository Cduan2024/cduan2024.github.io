Research Interest
My research interest lies in geometric representation theory and related combinatorics. In particular, I am very interested in the geometry of affine Springer fibers and (finite and affine) Weyl groups.
Publications and preprints

  • Good position braid representatives and transversal slices of unipotent orbits, arXiv:2310.12750.
  • On certain varieties attached to braid representatives of a Weyl group element, arXiv:2312.04798.
  • Partial order on braid elements and on unipotent orbits in reductive groups, in preparation.
